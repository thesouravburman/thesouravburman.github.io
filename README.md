## About

I am a third-year Computer Science Engineering student at **Brainware University, Kolkata** — specialising in AI and Computer Vision. I have built systems that detect eye conditions without any hardware, classify live traffic in real time, and read market sentiment from raw text.

I interned at **Samsung R&D Institute, Kolkata**, trained in **Cryptographic Security at the Indian Statistical Institute** under a MeitY government programme, and completed **Qualcomm's Deep Tech Entrepreneurship** programme with a perfect score.

I am not interested in average things. I am obsessed with the intersection of precision engineering and real human impact.
